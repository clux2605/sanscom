HR Services

Our HR Service package provides a fully outsourced HR service that can be tailored to fit with your business and help support delivery of your overall business strategy.

For a fixed monthly fee, you get a professional, high level HR support throughout the year and peace of mind that your HR needs are being fully supported, has updated policies and procedures and is compliant with the latest employment legislation.

We provide:

  • Redundancy, resignations and termination of employment services
  • Change Management
  • Health & Safety policy review, formulation and ongoing monitoring to ensure compliance with current Health & Safety Legislation
  • Absence Management
  • Provision of Job evaluations,
  • Establish minimum performance measurement standards and introduction of targets
  • Provide Staff Appraisals and Performance Management Review Processes
  • Support managers with staff appraisals and performance management of employees
  • Support Managers with Disciplinary Investigations / Hearings and required actions
  • Training Administration
